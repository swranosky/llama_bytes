📺 About Llama Bytes

Welcome to Llama Bytes, where fun meets foundational learning! We're on a mission to introduce kids and lifelong learners to the world of Artificial Intelligence and Language Models — all through animated characters, storytelling, and accessible explanations.

Whether you're a student, parent, or senior citizen, our goal is to make AI understandable, safe, and exciting!
